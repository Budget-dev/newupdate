# **App Name**: BudgetDev Agency Portal

## Core Features:

- Agency Landing Page: A prominent and dynamic landing page showcasing The BudgetDev's core value proposition and services, reflecting the visual style of the inspiration site.
- Services Showcase: Dedicated sections or pages to detail The BudgetDev's range of services, including web design, web apps, and custom software, similar to the Wibify structure.
- Portfolio & Case Studies: A feature to display past projects and client references effectively, highlighting successful digital solutions provided by the agency.
- Contact & Quote Request Form: An interactive form enabling potential clients to easily inquire about services and request a free quote, mirroring the 'KOstenlos Entwurf bekommen' functionality.
- Multi-language Support: Basic functionality to switch the website's content between multiple languages (e.g., English and German) for a broader audience.
- Blog Content Management & Display: An integrated system to create, manage, and display blog posts, allowing BudgetDev to share industry insights and agency news.
- AI-driven Content Draft Tool: An internal tool that uses generative AI to assist in drafting initial content for new service descriptions, blog post outlines, or portfolio entries based on user-provided keywords.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme to convey professionalism and clarity, inspired by the reference image's bright aesthetic.
- Primary color: A vibrant teal-green (#14E6BB) for highlights, underlines, and interactive elements, mirroring the 'digital success' accent from the screenshot. This choice embodies a modern, innovative digital presence without being literal.
- Background color: A very subtle cool off-white (#F8FAF9) that provides a clean canvas while subtly complementing the primary teal-green hue, maintaining visual continuity with the light scheme.
- Accent color: A sophisticated dark charcoal gray (#242827) for prominent text, headings, and buttons, ensuring high readability and a strong, modern contrast against the light background.
- Headline and body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly legible appearance, consistent with the sleek agency aesthetic seen in the inspiration.
- Minimalist, line-art style icons with rounded edges, as observed in the floating contact buttons and share options on the reference site. Icons should be consistently weighted.
- Clean, full-width responsive layout with generous negative space, subtle grid background patterns in hero sections, and prominent centered headings. Key navigational and contact elements are strategically placed in a floating header and fixed side elements.
- Subtle hover effects on navigation items and buttons, smooth transitions for section reveals on scroll, and elegant dropdown animations for menu elements, maintaining a polished user experience.
